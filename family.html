My Family

My Father

My father's name is Cheng-Lu John Hsu, but I just call him 爸爸, which means dad in Mandarin Chinese. He works at Oracle Corporation as a software engineer, and has never left the corporation. One of his hobbies is running. He has run many marathons, even ones out of the country, such as Taiwan.

My Mother

My mother's name is Fenju Alison Tsai, but I call her 媽媽, which is mom in Chinese. She currently works at Washington Hospital in Fremont, as a Speech Language Pathologist. She originally studied to become an architectural designer in college, but soon found passion in helping others as an SLP. I find her job special because there is a very low population of SLPs in the world.

My Brother

He practices daily, for hours, and attends a baseball tournament every two weeks, with his team. During the spring, he plays in Sunnyvale Little League, but he also joins a travel ball team, his friends' dads created. They started out as Stealth, but after hiring a baseball coach, they became the Lumberjacks, or Jacks.
